Brownies

Ingredients

1.25 AP
1 t salt


Chocolate sauce:
4 oz unsweet chocolate
2 oz bittersweet chocolate
2 sticks butter

1 cup sugar added once melted

once integrated, bring off heat, add
1 t vanilla extract

Mix together:
4 large eggs
1 cups sugar

half of this egg/sugar mix folds into the chocolate
whip the other half with mixer until tripled in size

fold this into the chocolate mix
integrate dry ingredients

bake on 350F for 20-25 minutes
